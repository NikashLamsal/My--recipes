Carrot Pudding

Description

Carrot pudding is a sweet, creamy dessert made with grated carrots, milk, sugar, and ghee. It's cooked until thickened, often flavored with cardamom and garnished with nuts and raisins, creating a rich and flavorful treat

Ingredients

  • 2 cups grated carrots
  • 2 cups full-fat milk
  • 1/4 cup sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 2 tablespoons ghee (clarified butter)
  • 1/4 teaspoon cardamom powder
  • 1/4 cup chopped nuts (cashews, almonds)
  • A pinch of saffron (optional)

Steps

  1. Cook Carrots: Saut√© grated carrots in ghee for 2-3 minutes.
  2. Add Milk: Pour in milk and cook until the mixture thickens.
  3. Sweeten: Add sugar and cardamom powder, stirring until dissolved.
  4. Garnish: Add nuts and raisins, and cook for a few more minutes.
  5. vServe: Serve warm, garnished with extra nuts if desired.
